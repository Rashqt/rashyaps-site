FAQ

Common questions

The things people usually wonder before reaching out.

Do you take freelance projects?

Yes — mostly web dev, personal brand sites, and design-adjacent work. I'm selective but happy to hear what you're building.

Can I republish or share your writing?

Sharing is always welcome — just credit RashYaps and link back. For republishing elsewhere, reach out first and I'll almost certainly say yes.

Do you collaborate with other creators?

Yes, if the work feels aligned and genuine. I'm not interested in collab-for-reach — but if you're making something real, let's talk.

How quickly do you respond?

Usually within 48 hours. Sometimes longer if I'm deep in a project. I always reply to genuine messages — just give it a couple of days.

Are you open to writing commissions?

Occasionally — depends entirely on the topic. I don't write content I wouldn't put my name on proudly. Reach out with what you have in mind.

What's the Discord community like?

Small, calm, and intentional. Conversations about creativity, building things, and the internet — without the performance of most online spaces.
