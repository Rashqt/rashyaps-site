FAQ

Common questions

Do you take freelance projects?

Yes — mostly web dev, personal brand sites, and design work. I'm selective but happy to hear what you're building.

Can I share your writing?

Sharing is always welcome — just credit Rashqyiiiiee / RashYaps and link back.

Do you collaborate with creators?

Yes, if the work feels aligned and genuine. Not interested in collab-for-reach — but if you're making something real, let's talk.

How quickly do you respond?

Usually within 48 hours. Sometimes longer if I'm deep in a project. Always reply to genuine messages.

Are you open to writing commissions?

Occasionally — depends on the topic. I don't write content I wouldn't put my name on proudly.

What's the Discord like?

Small, calm, and intentional. Conversations about creativity, building, and the internet — without the performance of most online spaces.
